Welcome to Quick Cards

Quick cards is a quick referrence for monsters and spells in Dungeons and Dragons 5e.

Search for a monster or spell name, or even a partial search like 'as'. Select from monsters and spells and hit search.

If you are just browsing, leave the search field blank for a full listing.

Please note that it can take a few moments to display the search results for a full listing or for partial search terms, since they can sometimes return hundreds of matches.
